\section{Experimental Setup}

\paragraph{Benchmark.} LIBERO-Spatial~\cite{libero_paper}: 10 tabletop manipulation tasks, MuJoCo with EGL rendering, 300-timestep episodes, binary success.

\paragraph{Base policies.} (i) \textbf{OpenVLA-7B OFT}: autoregressive VLA, 4096-d hidden states, one action per step. (ii) \textbf{ACT}: CVAE action-chunking, 256-d hidden states, 8-step chunks.

\paragraph{Safety probe.} 3${\times}$256 hidden layers, GELU, Dropout 0.3, LayerNorm input. 108K params (ACT) / 1.1M (OpenVLA). AdamW, early stopping on val AUC.

\paragraph{Modes.} Each task: $N{=}20$ episodes per mode. \textbf{vanilla}: unmodified. \textbf{latent\_stop}: freeze action if $\sigma(\hat{y}^{\mathrm{fail}}){\ge}\tau_{\mathrm{stop}}$. \textbf{latent\_jiggle}: random-direction correction, matched magnitude. \textbf{mppi}: sampling-based planning using failure head as cost. \textbf{steering} (ours): full PULSE pipeline.

\paragraph{Controller.} $\alpha{=}0.2$, $\beta{=}0.3$, $c_{\max}{=}0.004$\,m, $\tau_c{=}0.003$\,m, $\tau_f{=}0.6$, MPPI: 64 samples, $\sigma{=}0.003$\,m.

\paragraph{Generalization.} Strict zero-shot (train 8 tasks, eval 2 held-out) and disturbance OOD (push magnitude 0.08).

\paragraph{Statistics.} Two-proportion z-tests, Fisher exact, TOST ($\pm$2\,pp), Holm correction, Pearson/Spearman correlations. All $p$-values two-sided.

\paragraph{Physical robot evaluation.}
\label{sec:physical_setup}
We evaluate on two robot arms: a Kinova Gen3 Lite (6-DoF) and a UFactory xArm 6 (6-DoF), each with a parallel gripper and wrist-mounted RGB camera. A GPU server runs the base policy and safety probe, sending commands to the arm over network. The safety probe is trained exclusively on simulation hidden states and deployed without fine-tuning---testing whether the latent safety manifold transfers from sim to real. Tasks: tabletop pick-and-place matching LIBERO-Spatial semantics. Modes: vanilla, steering, latent stop. 50 episodes per mode per task, with deterministic resets and blind success labeling by an independent annotator. Safety interlocks: workspace bounding ($\pm$50\,mm/step), velocity saturation, and e-stop. Measures: success rate, failure AUC on real trajectories (hand-labeled), intervention rate, and gating pattern comparison to simulation.

\paragraph{Compute.} HPC cluster, NVIDIA A100 (40\,GB), $\sim$2,000 GPU-hours total.
