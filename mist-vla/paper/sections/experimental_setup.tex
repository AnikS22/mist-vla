\section{Experimental Setup}
\label{sec:experimental_setup}

\paragraph{Benchmark.} LIBERO-Spatial~\citep{libero_paper}: 10 tabletop manipulation tasks in MuJoCo, 300-step episodes, binary success (goal predicate fires $\Rightarrow$ success). Success and failure rollouts are shown in Fig.~\ref{fig:rollout_filmstrip}.

\paragraph{Policies.} \textbf{OpenVLA-7B OFT}~\citep{openvla_paper}: autoregressive VLA, 4096-d last-token hidden state. \textbf{ACT}~\citep{act_paper}: CVAE action-chunking, 256-d encoder hidden state, 8-step chunks; the correction is applied to the first chunk action.

\paragraph{Data.} OpenVLA pool: 200 success / 374 failure trajectories (19{,}440 / 82{,}280 steps). ACT pool: 499 success / 500 failure trajectories (59{,}328 / 150{,}000 steps). Trajectory-level 75/15/10 train/val/test split, stratified by task and label (\texttt{np.random.seed(42)}); all probe metrics on the trajectory-disjoint test set.

\paragraph{Controller modes.} $N{=}20$ episodes/mode/task. \textbf{Vanilla}: unmodified base policy. \textbf{K-sample MPPI}: $K{=}16$ feature-space perturbations scored by the PULSE failure head (softmin $\tau{=}5.0$, $\sigma{=}0.01$). \textbf{Single-step gating (Ours)}: one failure-head query per step. \textbf{Latent Jiggle}: identical to single-step gating but with correction direction replaced by a random unit vector at matched magnitude---isolates the contribution of the correction head (\S\ref{sec:mechanism}). Hyperparameters swept: $\alpha \in \{0.10, 0.12, 0.15, 0.18\}$, $c_{\max} \in \{0.003, 0.0035, 0.004\}$\,m, $\tau_f \in \{0.55, 0.60, 0.65\}$; pooled table averages over sweep (modal: $\alpha{=}0.12$, $c_{\max}{=}0.003$\,m, $\tau_f{=}0.65$).

\paragraph{Statistics.} Two-proportion $z$-tests and TOST equivalence (${\pm}2$\,pp), Holm-corrected over the primary contrast family \{single-step vs.\ MPPI, single-step vs.\ vanilla, MPPI vs.\ vanilla\}. Wilson 95\% CIs in Table~\ref{tab:final_pooled_results}. All $p$-values two-sided. Pearson and Spearman correlations reported for the adaptive-gating analysis.

\paragraph{Compute.} NVIDIA A100 (40\,GB), ${\approx}2{,}000$ GPU-hours total for rollout collection, probe training, and controller evaluation.
